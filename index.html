Solar made simple.

Although all our work is completely bespoke giving you the most beneficial and cost effective solar conversion, we have ensured our process remains simple, clear and most of all… personal.

intial scoping call

Lets talk about you

We prefer to talk to our customers and understand their exact situation. Its important for us to understand your goals and get the details we need to come equipped with various ideas for your property when we book our visit.

FACE TO FACE ASSESSMENT

Visit and Assess

Unlike other solar providers, we believe that visiting each property is critical. It allows us to assess suitability and design a bespoke solar system that maximises efficiency and meets your specific energy needs.

PLAN + PRESENT

Present your bespoke, tailored plan

Rather than offering standard packages, we create tailored solutions that genuinely work for each property. If a system is not right for a customer, we will say so. We will break down your plan with transparency in mind, allowing you to understand all the elements that will turn you green and how long before you will start earning.

HANDOVER TO INSTALLATION

Book your installation date.

Provided you are confident and happy with our plan, we will look to book the installation date with our experienced teams. We will stay close to the entire process to ensure we adhere to our joint plan and quality is kept to the highest of standards. We will always give you an opportunity to voice any concerns and look forward to hearing the great feedback from you at the end!
